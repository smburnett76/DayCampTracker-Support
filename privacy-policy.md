# Camp Track Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 23, 2026

Camp Track (“**we**,” “**our**,” or “**the app**”) is a summer camp scheduling app for parents. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Camp Track iOS application (“**Camp Track**”).

**Summary:** Camp Track is designed to keep your family’s schedule on your iPhone. We do not operate servers that store your camp data, and we do not sell your information.

---

## 1. Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to anyone who downloads and uses Camp Track from the Apple App Store. Camp Track is intended for **parents and guardians** managing household schedules. It is not directed at children to use on their own.

---

## 2. Information stored on your device

When you use Camp Track, you may enter and store the following **on your device only**:

- **Children’s profiles** — such as a display name, color, and optional emoji avatar  
- **Camp details** — camp name, location/address, dates, times, notes, checklist items, and registration status  
- **Assignments** — which children are assigned to which camps  
- **App preferences** — such as calendar display mode, default maps app, and reminder on/off settings  

This information is saved locally (for example, in the app’s on-device storage) and is used to show your calendar, dashboard, Today view, maps, and camp reminders.

**We do not receive, access, or store this schedule data on our servers** because Camp Track does not use a backend account or cloud database operated by us.

---

## 3. Information we do not collect

Camp Track **does not**:

- Require you to create an account or sign in  
- Transmit your schedule data to us for storage or analytics  
- Use advertising SDKs or sell your data  
- Collect payment information (the app does not process purchases in-app as described in this policy)  

If we introduce features that change this (for example, optional cloud sync), we will update this policy before or when those features ship.

---

## 4. Device permissions and features

### Local notifications

If you enable camp reminders, Camp Track schedules **local notifications** on your device (for example, one week before, the day before, and the morning a confirmed camp starts). These alerts are created and delivered by iOS on your phone; we do not send push notifications from our own servers.

You can turn reminders off in the app (Home → bell icon) or in **Settings → Notifications → Camp Track**.

### Location and addresses

Camp Track does **not** track your real-time GPS location.

When you enter a camp **address or place name**, the app may send that text to **Apple’s location services** (such as MapKit search and geocoding) to show camps on a map. That processing is subject to [Apple’s Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

When you tap **Open in Maps**, Camp Track opens your chosen maps app (Apple Maps, Google Maps, or Waze, if installed). Those apps handle data under their own policies.

---

## 5. Information about children

Parents and guardians enter information about their children (such as a first name) to organize camps. **You control what you enter.** That information stays on your device unless you choose to share it outside the app (for example, via a screenshot or device backup).

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 through Camp Track. If you believe a child has provided us information in error, contact us using the details in Section 10.

---

## 6. Backups and device security

Your data may be included in **encrypted device backups** (for example, iCloud Backup or a computer backup) according to your Apple ID and device settings. We do not control those backups.

Protect your device with a passcode or Face ID / Touch ID. Anyone who can unlock your phone can access data stored in Camp Track.

---

## 7. Data retention and deletion

- **While installed:** Your schedule remains on your device until you edit or delete it in the app.  
- **Delete the app:** Removing Camp Track from your iPhone typically deletes the app’s local data from that device. Data may still exist in device backups until those backups are replaced or removed.  
- **No remote copy:** Because we do not host your schedule data, we cannot delete it from our servers on your behalf.

---

## 8. Security

We rely on Apple’s iOS app sandbox to isolate Camp Track data from other apps. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, but keeping data on-device and not operating a central database reduces exposure from server breaches.

---

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy with a new “Last updated” date. Continued use of Camp Track after changes means you accept the updated policy.
